Policies

Currently in progress

If you encounter any problems with your transaction or if you have any questions about the status of your order, simply contact us: info@seoulcaliber.com

All orders are shipped promptly from our warehouse or in-store inventory within 3 – 10 business days using UPS, FedEx, or USPS. Tracking numbers are available for items shipped via UPS and FedEx.

We will provide all descriptions for used items. All descriptions for used items are honest and based on actual items being offered for sale. Items being sold in this manner will be marked as such. All sales on used items are final. Photos will be provided when available.

Other items available for sale not listed on this website include discontinued parts or parts for firearms no longer produced. Please contact us if you have any questions regarding parts for firearms no longer produced All items on this website are subject to change without notice. This includes item availability, one-of-a-kind items, and pricing.

All internet and private party transfers are CASH only.
Tanigble items (Firearms, Magazines, Ammunition, Accessories) paid with credit card are subject to an additional processing fee with ID required.
Tanigble items (Firearms, Magazines, Ammunition, Accessories) transactions are subject to local sales tax.
Gunsmith and Servicing is not subject to local sales tax.

All firearms, magazines, receivers and restricted law enforcement items are sold and shipped in accordance with all existing federal, state and local laws and regulations. Many of the firearms, magazines and parts for sale on this website may be restricted or prohibited in your area. Please check your local and state regulations before ordering. You must be 18 or older to purchase a long gun and 21 or older to purchase a handgun per ATF rules, but state and local laws may differ and you will need to check your local laws before purchasing a firearm.
All new firearms are shipped in the original manufacturer’s box, with magazine(s), accessories and applicable warranty. Firearms requiring warranty work must be returned to the factory within 1 year of purchase for repair. Accessories and special order items not normally sold by our store will not be covered under warranty even if these products are part of an integrated firearms package, unless we have guaranteed the entire package in writing. Please contact the original manufacturer for warranty information on all accessories and special order items.

Transfers

See an awesome deal or sale online and need it transferred locally? Ship it to us!
Please contact us prior to placing an online order. Shoot us a text to receive a reply ASAP
If you place an order before contacting us or receiving a confirmation, an additional fee may be applied.
Pricing:

    Long guns (rifle and shotguns), handguns, frames, receivers: $50 for first transfer
    Additional firearms (within the same transaction) are $10 each

    We accept transfers from non licensees (indivuals). However we do not release our FFL to anyone that is not a licensee. You may verify our FFL by using the ATF FFL EZ CHECK WEBSITE and entering 5-84-17025.
    Each individual must bring valid identification.
    We can provide a notarized Bill of Sale for an additional cost.
Please contact us to schedule an appointment to come in and process the private transfer.
       
Note: Per Colorado HB23-1219, Waiting Period To Deliver A Firearm, Purchaser will be unable to take delivery of firearm(s) a minimum of 3 days from the time and date of completed CBI background check
