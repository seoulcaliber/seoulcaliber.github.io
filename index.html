Transfers

Please contact us prior to placing an online order.
If you place an order before contacting us and receiving a confirmation, an additional fee may be applied.

We accept transfers from non licensees (indivuals). However we do not release our FFL to anyone that is not a licensee. You may verify our FFL by using the ATF FFL EZ CHECK WEBSITE and entering 5-84-17025. Each individual must bring valid identification. We can provide a notarized Bill of Sale for an additional cost.
Please contact us to schedule an appointment to come in and process the private transfer.
Note: Per Colorado HB23-1219, Waiting Period To Deliver A Firearm, Purchaser will be unable to take delivery of firearm(s) a minimum of 3 days from the time and date of completed CBI background check
